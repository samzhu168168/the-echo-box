Editorial policy

Our content is practical support, not professional advice.

The Echo Box writes for people who are tempted to send an emotional message after a breakup. Our content focuses on pause, privacy, no-contact structure, and factual communication when contact is unavoidable.

How content is created

Search intent firstPages answer one specific breakup reset or no-contact question.
Visible safety boundariesWe label what the tool cannot do and point unsafe situations away from the website.
No fake authorityWe do not present the site as therapy, legal advice, or medical advice.
No fake reviewsWe do not use invented ratings, testimonials, or outcomes.

Review rules

Before publishing, each page should pass three checks: no private user text is exposed, the safety disclaimer is clear, and claims are either product facts or linked to a source page.
