About

The Echo Box is built for the moment before you text.

The Echo Box is a private browser-based breakup reset. It helps you write the message without sending it, wait ten minutes, track no contact, and keep a Reality Box of facts your nervous system may forget during an emotional spike.

The product is intentionally small. No login. No social feed. No AI chat. No promise that a website can fix grief, attachment, safety, or legal problems. The job is to create a pause and give you one calmer next action.

Principles

What we optimize for

Privacy firstPrivate message text stays in your browser unless you export it.
Small actionsThe tool gives you a pause, not a diagnosis or a life plan.
Clear boundariesWe separate emotional urges from necessary factual contact.
Plain languageContent is written to be used in a hard moment, not to sound clinical.

This is not crisis support.

If you may hurt yourself, hurt someone else, or if someone may hurt you, contact local emergency services or a trusted person near you now.
