Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

1. What The Echo Box does

The Echo Box helps you create a family emergency binder with contacts, medications, doctors, insurance notes, bill reminders, home instructions, care preferences, and family messages.

2. Information you type into the builder

The emergency binder builder runs in your browser. The text you type is saved locally in your browser storage so you can continue editing. We do not operate a database for storing your binder content, and we do not receive the text you enter into the builder.

3. Payments

If you purchase a paid kit, payment is processed by Gumroad or another third-party checkout provider. We do not store your credit card details.

4. Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to understand aggregate site usage, such as page views and referral sources. This helps us improve the product.

5. Sensitive information

Do not enter full passwords, full account secrets, Social Security numbers, or other highly sensitive credentials into the builder. The Echo Box is an organization tool, not a secure password manager.
