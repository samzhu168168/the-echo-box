Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 17, 2026

The Echo Box (operating as The Echo Box, LoveScribe, and FutureBloom) ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We prioritize user privacy by minimizing data collection. Our core service operates primarily on your device (client-side), meaning we do not see or store the content you create unless you utilize specific premium services.

A. Information You Provide to Us

  • Payment Information: If you purchase a product, our third-party payment processors (Gumroad or Stripe) collect your email address and payment details to process the transaction. We do not store your credit card information on our servers.
  • "Executor's Trust" Service Data: If you purchase the "Executor's Trust" upsell service, we explicitly collect and store: The message content, the recipient's email address, and the scheduled delivery date. This data is stored solely for the purpose of fulfilling the future delivery service.

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous information about how visitors interact with our site. This includes data such as browser type, device type, and pages visited. This data is aggregated and does not identify individual users.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Services: To process payments and deliver digital goods via our payment processors.
  • To Fulfill "Executor's Trust" Orders: To securely store and send messages at the scheduled future date.
  • To Improve Our Website: To analyze anonymous traffic data and optimize the user experience.
  • To Communicate: To send transaction receipts or important service updates related to your purchase.

3. Data Storage and Security

Standard Service: For our standard certificate creation tools, all data processing occurs locally in your browser. We do not store your text or certificates on our servers.

"Executor's Trust": For this specific service, we employ industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect the stored messages and recipient details until the delivery date.

Third-Party Processors: Transactions are secured by Gumroad or Stripe, who adhere to PCI-DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard) requirements.

4. Third-Party Services

We may share data with trusted third-party service providers solely for operational needs: Google Analytics, Gumroad / Stripe. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others.
