# 90-Day Growth Plan

Date: 2026-07-10

## North star

Turn cold social traffic into a free reset start, then into paid kit checkout intent.

## Days 1-14: infrastructure and proof

- Verify Google Search Console.
- Verify Bing Webmaster Tools.
- Deploy current trust pages and product page.
- Confirm Gumroad UTM tracking.
- Publish 2-3 short videos per day using the strongest hooks.
- Track visits, reset starts, paid CTA clicks, and Gumroad product views.

## Days 15-45: high-intent SEO pages

Create the six high-intent pages only after this infrastructure batch is stable:

- `should-i-text-my-ex.html`
- `no-contact-calculator.html`
- `i-broke-no-contact.html`
- `he-texted-me-during-no-contact.html`
- `my-ex-liked-my-story.html`
- `stop-checking-my-ex-social-media.html`

Each page should include one useful interaction, a safety boundary, and a paid kit CTA.

## Days 46-75: content compounding

- Build short-video clusters around the six high-intent questions.
- Turn winning X posts into short scripts.
- Add internal links from SEO pages to the product page.
- Review Search Console queries weekly.

## Days 76-90: conversion tightening

- Improve paid-kit section based on CTA click data.
- Add one free download only if Gumroad or email capture is explicitly approved.
- Test product page headline and price framing.
- Keep the site static unless real traffic proves a need for heavier tooling.

## Channel priority

1. TikTok / YouTube Shorts: fastest creative testing.
2. X: fast copy testing and direct pain hooks.
3. SEO pages: slow but durable demand capture.
4. Reddit/Pinterest: only after the core site and measurement are stable.
