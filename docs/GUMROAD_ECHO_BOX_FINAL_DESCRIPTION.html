How the 30-day plan works

Each day gives you one small action, one prompt, and one practical way to protect the pause. The goal is not to write pages for hours. The goal is to make the next ten minutes cleaner, then repeat that structure for thirty days.

Emergency 10-Minute Reset

Use this first when the urge is immediate. It gives you a short sequence to put the phone down, name the trigger, and delay the decision until your body is calmer.

Reality Box prompts

The Reality Box helps you save the facts you forget when you only remember the good moments. It is designed for private self-reflection and can be used alongside the free Echo Box web tool.

No Contact Restart Plan

If you already texted, checked, replied, or reopened the loop, the restart plan helps you make the next choice clean instead of treating the whole month as ruined.

Necessary Communication Scripts

Sometimes contact is required for logistics, shared responsibilities, work, money, or formal issues. The scripts keep those messages short, factual, and limited.

Social Media Checking Plan

The kit includes a simple plan for reducing profile checks, story checks, and indirect monitoring when you know it is keeping the loop alive.

Printable trackers

Use the trigger tracker and progress tracker to see patterns over time and notice what actually helps.
