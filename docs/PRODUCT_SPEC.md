# Product Spec - Breakup Reset MVP

## Positioning

The Echo Box is now a private breakup reset and no-contact tool for the moment before someone sends a message they may regret.

## MVP scope

Included in Batch A:

- Unsent Message box
- Ten-minute reset timer
- Trigger selector with short guidance
- No-contact counter
- Reality Box local notes
- Necessary contact filter
- Local export and delete controls
- Safety and privacy pages

## Explicit non-goals

- No therapy promise
- No crisis support claim
- No legal, medical, or financial advice
- No AI dependency
- No paid checkout until offer and Gumroad copy are reviewed
- No false testimonials or outcome guarantees

## Primary conversion hypothesis

A free private reset tool can capture breakup/no-contact traffic better than a family emergency binder homepage. Paid conversion should come later through a 30-day No Contact Kit, relapse scripts, and boundary templates.
