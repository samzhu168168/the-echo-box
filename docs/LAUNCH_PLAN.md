Deploy the free Breakup Reset homepage. Do not connect paid checkout yet. Publish 2 short videos and 2 X posts pointing to the free reset.

## Day 2

Review basic behavior manually: reset starts, no-contact counter, Reality Box saves, old family route still works. Draft Gumroad product copy for the 30-Day No Contact Kit.

Create paid Gumroad product only if the free page is stable. Suggested first price test: $9 to $19 for a digital recovery kit.

- "Do not send that message yet."
- "Put it here for 10 minutes first."
- "No login. No app. Just a private reset."

Provide Gumroad product URL and approved paid offer copy before checkout is added.
