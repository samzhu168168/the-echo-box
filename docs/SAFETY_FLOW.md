# Safety Flow

## Current safety handling

The homepage includes a visible safety note and a dedicated safety page. The Unsent Message flow checks for basic safety-related phrases and switches the timer guidance to a safety warning when triggered.

## Safety language

If the user may hurt themselves, hurt someone else, or may be harmed, the site tells them to contact local emergency services or a trusted person near them now.

## Boundary

The product is a pause and self-reflection tool. It is not therapy, crisis counseling, legal advice, domestic violence support, or emergency intervention.

## Next safety step

Owner should review the safety copy before paid traffic or large social distribution. If targeting sensitive breakup keywords, add crisis resources by country or keep copy generic and emergency-service oriented.
