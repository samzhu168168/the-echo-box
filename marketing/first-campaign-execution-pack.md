## Gemini Image Direction

Use this shared visual system across all 10 pins:

Vertical Pinterest pin, 1000x1500. Calm premium printable planner aesthetic. Warm off-white paper, muted teal, soft red, muted blue, and small gold accents. Clean organized desk or home command center scene. Large readable headline area. Realistic paper texture. No scary hospital imagery, no dramatic grief imagery, no fake legal or medical claims, no real account numbers or drug names.

## 7-Day Execution Plan

Day 1:

- Generate the first 10 images in Gemini.
- Publish Pins 1-5.
- Submit the new page and sitemap in Google Search Console.

Day 2:

- Publish Pins 6-10.
- Update Gumroad cover, description, and delivery copy.

Day 3:

- Create 5 visual variants from the best-looking prompts.
- Post 1 X thread linking to the aging parents guide.

Day 4:

- Publish 5 more Pinterest variants.
- Comment on 20 relevant caregiver/aging parent posts without hard selling.

Day 5:

- Review Pinterest outbound clicks and saves.
- Make 5 more pins using the top 2 cover lines.

Day 6:

- Publish the next 5 pins.
- Rewrite any description that has impressions but no clicks.

Day 7:

- Fill the tracker.
- Keep the top 3 angles.
- Cut the bottom 3 angles.

## Decision Rule

If the first 7 days show clicks but no Gumroad purchases, do not change price first. Improve Gumroad cover, PDF previews, delivery explanation, and refund copy first.
