# Gumroad Product Page

## Product name

The Echo Box - 30-Day No Contact Reset Kit

## One-line selling point

A private, practical 30-day plan for the moments when you want to text, check, or go back.

## Short description

Use one small action each day to pause before emotional contact, rebuild no-contact structure, and remember the facts your nervous system forgets.

## Full product details

The Echo Box - 30-Day No Contact Reset Kit is a digital guide for the hard moments after a breakup: late-night texting urges, profile checking, closure loops, surprise messages, and restarts after breaking no contact.

It is practical and short by design. Most days take less than five minutes.

## What is included

- 30 days of short practical resets
- Emergency 10-minute reset plan
- Reality Box prompts
- No-contact restart plan
- Necessary communication scripts
- Social-media checking plan
- Printable trigger and progress trackers
- Access instructions for the free Echo Box web tool

## Good fit for

- People trying not to send emotional messages
- People restarting no contact after a slip
- People who need practical structure, not long journaling
- People who want one small daily action

## Not a good fit for

- Anyone seeking therapy, crisis support, medical care, legal advice, or domestic violence support
- Anyone looking for a get-your-ex-back system
- Anyone looking for manipulation scripts
- Anyone in immediate danger

## How to use

Open the free Echo Box tool first. Put the message there. Start the ten-minute reset. Then use the daily plan when you need more structure.

## Privacy note

The website tool stores notes locally in your browser. The digital kit is a downloadable file. Do not enter passwords, government IDs, banking details, or other high-risk secrets.

## Disclaimer

This product is a self-reflection and organization tool. It is not therapy, medical treatment, legal advice, crisis support, or a guarantee of healing or no contact.

## Refund policy suggestion

Because this is a digital product delivered immediately, refunds are generally not offered after download. If a file is missing or broken, contact support and it will be fixed.
