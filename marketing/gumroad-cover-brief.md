# Gumroad Cover Brief

## Visual positioning

Quiet, private, midnight, warm neutral, minimal, premium but not luxurious, feminine but not childish.

## Size suggestions

- Gumroad cover: 1280 x 720
- Square thumbnail: 1080 x 1080
- Social preview: 1200 x 630

## Main title

The Echo Box

## Subtitle

30-Day No Contact Reset Kit

## Composition

A dim bedside table, closed phone face down, small notebook, warm lamp edge, and a simple sealed box shape. The mood should feel calm and private, not dramatic.

## Font direction

Use an editorial serif for the main title and a clean sans-serif for details. Avoid playful bubble fonts.

## Color direction

Deep charcoal, warm ivory, muted brass, soft brown gray, optional dark teal accent.

## Forbidden elements

- Crying woman close-up
- Broken red heart
- Loud pink palette
- Get-your-ex-back language
- Ex-partner character
- Therapist or medical imagery
- Manipulation cues

## Canva steps

1. Create 1280 x 720 canvas.
2. Add dark warm background.
3. Place notebook and phone image or generated scene.
4. Add title in large serif.
5. Add subtitle under title.
6. Add footer: Private. Practical. 30 days.
7. Export PNG.
8. Crop a square thumbnail version.

## Image generation prompt

A quiet midnight bedside table, phone face down, small neutral notebook, warm lamp light, a simple closed keepsake box, calm private breakup recovery mood, premium minimal editorial product photography, warm neutral colors, deep charcoal background, no people, no hearts, no medical imagery, no text.

Text: Do not text yet. Small footer: 30-Day Reset Kit.
