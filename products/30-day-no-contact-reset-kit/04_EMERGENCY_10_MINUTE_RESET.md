# Emergency 10-Minute Reset

## Minute 0
Paste the message into The Echo Box. Do not send it.

## Minutes 1-2
Name the trigger: text, loneliness, social media, anger, alcohol, memory, closure, or something else.

## Minutes 3-5
Read one Reality Box fact. If you have not written one yet, write: "One message can restart a loop I am trying to leave."

## Minutes 6-8
Do one body action: water, stand up, step outside, shower, or move the phone away.

## Minutes 9-10
Choose one option: send nothing, save the note, or use a factual logistics script if contact is truly necessary.

Do not make a life decision during an emotional spike.
