# Safety and Limits

The Echo Box is not crisis care, professional counseling, legal advice, medical advice, or domestic violence support.

Use qualified support if:

- You may hurt yourself.
- You may hurt someone else.
- Someone may hurt you.
- You are being stalked or threatened.
- Childcare, money, housing, legal issues, or safety are involved.

For co-parenting, work, money, documents, or safety, use short factual communication. Do not use the kit to avoid required responsibilities.
