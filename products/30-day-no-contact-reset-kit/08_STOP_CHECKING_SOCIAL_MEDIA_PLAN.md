# Stop Checking Social Media Plan

Checking feels like information. Often it is a trigger loop.

## Friction steps

- Move the app off the home screen.
- Mute or hide their account where possible.
- Add one extra login step.
- Set a one-hour no-checking block.
- Replace checking with a ten-minute ordinary action.

## Checking question

"What decision will this information help me make?"

If the answer is none, do not check.
