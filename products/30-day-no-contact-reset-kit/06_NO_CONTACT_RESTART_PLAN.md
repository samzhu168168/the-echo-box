# No-Contact Restart Plan

Breaking no contact does not mean the whole reset failed.

## If you sent one message

1. Do not send the second message to explain the first.
2. Open The Echo Box.
3. Restart the counter.
4. Write what triggered the contact.
5. Choose the next clean ten minutes.

## If they replied

Read it later if possible. A reply is not a command. If the topic is not logistics, safety, childcare, work, money, or documents, you can wait.

Restart sentence: "I contacted them. I do not need to turn one message into a full return."
