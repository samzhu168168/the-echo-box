# Necessary Communication Scripts

Use these only when contact is practical, not emotional.

## Childcare
"I can handle pickup at 5:30 on Friday. Please confirm by noon tomorrow."

## Work
"For the project handoff, please send the file by 3 PM. I will keep this thread limited to work logistics."

## Money or property
"I am available to coordinate the bill or property issue by email. Please send the amount, date, and document needed."

## Safety
If someone is unsafe or threatened, contact local emergency services or a trusted person near you. Do not rely on this script.

## Boundary line
"I am not discussing the relationship in this thread."
