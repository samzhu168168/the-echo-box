# Reality Box Prompts

- What made me feel small, anxious, or unsafe?
- What pattern did I keep explaining away?
- What happened after the apology?
- What did my body know before I admitted it?
- What would I tell a friend in this same situation?
- What am I hoping one message will fix?
- What does the calm version of me already know?
- What boundary protects tomorrow morning?
- What proof do I have that the loop repeats?
- What would make contact necessary, not emotional?
